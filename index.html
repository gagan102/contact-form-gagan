About Us

Who We Are

SGH Engineering established in 2010, is a leading manufacturer, supplier and distributor of optimum quality Fittings and Adaptors. We provide extensive range of premium quality products, precisely manufactured confirming set Industry Standards. Superior grade raw materials are selected from authentic vendors to produce precision engineered products by highly skilled professionals using cutting-edge technology. These fine-finished products are then rigorously checked against the required parameters by our quality analysts for any flaws. The customer oriented approach allows us to cater their specific needs and provide them with bespoke solutions in a stipulated amount of time. The ethical business policies paved a way for our business to grab significant customer base in the last decade.

How We Are Better

  • Raw Material

    High standard mild steel is the basis of our fittings. Bearing High Tensile Strength and High Impact Strength, a Permium Grade mild steel is the wisest choice for fine fittings.

  • Precision CNC Turning

  • Applications
